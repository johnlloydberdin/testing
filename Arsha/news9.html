GWT Training Concluded, 22 Participants Graduated

A total of 22 participants received a Certificate of Completion in the recently completed Government Web Template (GWT) training using the WordPress Content Management System, held at DICT Training Center, Post Office Building from October 17-21, 2022. The said activity was a partnership between DICT-Caraga and the Provincial Government of Agusan del Norte.

The simulation aims to build a safety culture in case of disaster and prepare provincial employees on proper evacuation routes and protocols in the event of an earthquake.

As the training concluded, the participants were tasked to present their respective Government Web Template Websites as part of the culmination of the five days training. Through this assessment, the participants could show how they have applied the skills and knowledge they acquired from the said activity. “I'm not a programmer and I don't know how to develop a website either, but in five days I learned these things using WordPress” according to Mr. Barry Lim of LGU Buenavista as he presented his Website as his output.

Moreover, the unforgettable shared experiences they had at the training were the camaraderie among with them. “It seems I am the youngest among the participants I am a neophyte in the government and IT industry, yet my fellow participants treat me nice, seems they're like my older brothers, they're easy to approach,” Mr. Mark Laurence Mejo of LGU Nasipit said as he gives his impression during the training. One of the representatives from LGU Las Nieves has expressed his intentions, he said he would tell their Mayor that the Website he created in the said training be the one to be applied to the DICT to become the official website of their municipality.

All the graduates were grateful to DICT-Caraga and also to Governor Ma. Angelica Rosedell M. Amante of Agusan del Norte for their continuing support for the fulfillment of the said activity. They also thanked Engr. Roland Taganahan, Engr Jhelo Tutor, and their colleagues as their facilitators.
